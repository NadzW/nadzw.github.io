    Punch-Em-Up

    Solo | Game Developer | February 2021 | Unity

    Summary

    This was the first game I ever made to "completion" and published to be played. Made in under 72 hours, following the limitations of only upto 5 colours and the theme power. You play as a street brawler and make your way through a street littered with thugs. No one can stop you from reaching the Thug Leader at the end of the street.

    This project served as my gateway into actually wanting to continue learning game development and eventually game design in more depth. Although it was a buggy mess, I gained confidence in my ability and skill to make great things!

    Skills

    • Game and combat design
    • Level Scripting / C#
    • Working under time crunch
